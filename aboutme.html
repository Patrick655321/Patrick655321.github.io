So, who am I?

With nearly 20 years experience in hospitality I feel it is time to leave the industry and have so decided my next adventure will be in to the wild world of coding. Currently completing a Coder Academy bootcamp, I aim to be ready to break out into the real world within 12 months.
I have always had an interest in learning and problem solving so it seems like a natural progression, right? Anyway, enough about my motivations, let's talk about what makes me me.
My hobbies include rock climbing, motorcycles, reading, writing and fitness. Taht's all well and good but what I really love is spending time out having dinner with friends. Perhaps a throwback to my hospo roots, I enjoy the decadence of a good meal and what I find more nourishing than even that is the company of those I hold dear.
I enjoy all kinds of music, although my go-to coding music is slow blues. Floating on beautiful melodies, riding the peaks of those soulful guitar licks and feeling the depths of those gravelly voices gives me a certain feeling of flow taht I find conducive to a good session at my PC.
