What have I been up to?

For the past almost 20 years I have been a hospitality worker and occasional body modifier. While I have stepped away from the body modification game for the time being while I focus on other ventures you can still see a small sample of my work here.

In the hospitality industry I have worked for some heavy hitters such as The Lucas Group and Retail Savvy Group. I also have had the good fortune to work for some more bespoke venues such as Hawthorn Lounge in New Zealand, which took out the award for Cocktail Experience of the Year in 2018 with me as the GM, as well as The Burlesque Bar (Closed) where I originally cut my teeth in cocktails, sound and lighting.

A bartender with a penchant for entertaining and a creative streak that extends beyond cocktails, I found myself also taking care of a lot of stock control for The Lucas Group and as such developed an interest in how the programs worked. Realising a growing passion for problem solving and streamlining processes, I have finally made the leap into coding!

For a more detailed and extensive list of my work experience please check out my resume and feel free to email me with any questions.

Work at the ASRC

One of the best decisions I have made in recent years was to join the ranks of volunteers engaged with The Asylum Seeker Resource Center, preparing hto meals and occasionally packing food for the members.
An organisation that has been around for over 20 years, the ASRC is Australia's largest human rights organisation for refugees and asylum seekers. I am but a small cog in this very big vehicle, however this work and the people I have met through the organisation have been a source of great inspiration for my day to day. If you haven't checked them out yet I'd strongly recommend you have a look at what they're up to! Please see the link above or if you have any questions about what i do specifically feel free to contact me to discuss.
