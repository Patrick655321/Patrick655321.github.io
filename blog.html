The goings on in a bit more detail...

Below is a history of blog posts of some of the fun and interesting things I have gotten up to. This is not specifically limited to work, however will be pretty work-centric as I do thoroughly enjoy the things I do.

A Completed Winter Menu

01-Jun-2022

The winter menu is finally complete and ready to launch! With these gorgeous promo pics taken, recipes finalized and stock arriving in the next week get ready to imbibe at Allie-Pat's Bar and Grill this winter! Featuring (From left to right):

  • Gluhwein
  • Blackforest Brule
  • Blood Orange Sour
  • Georgeous Old Fashioned
  • Liquid Biscoff
  • Alpine Spritz
  • Rhubarb Caprioska
  • Heaven's Glitter
  • Hot Buttered Rum
The Hot Apple Strudel Blazer will also be on the menu, but you have to come in and see that for yourself!

Winter Menu is coming!

31-Apr-2022

With winter approaching here is a sneak peek of some of our upcoming cocktails. We have A spiced hot-buttered rum to keep you warm, then Heaven's Glitter: Lemon Myrtle infused Gin with Strega, citus and a house made lemon sherbert rim, Gorgeous Old Fashioned is an alpine inspired take on a classic with Rye Whiskey, Amaro Monetenegro and Fernet Branca and finally a Blackforest Brulee with VSOP Cognac, Creme de mure, egg, cream and a hard caramel lid.

Learning to cook!

31-Feb-2022

So I have been at the ASRC for a while now but due to COVID19 the community kitchen has been closed. Well, I am pleased to say we are back open and cooking with gas! Aliyah has been in teaching us how to make traditional Palestinian dishes from her home and wowsers, what a treat! With an eggplant moussaka, tahini chicken, daal and some jewelled rice we eat well these days.
